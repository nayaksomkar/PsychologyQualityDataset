# Psychology Quality Dataset

A curated, high-quality dataset for training AI models on psychology and mental health conversations. Cleaned and filtered from multiple public HuggingFace datasets, optimized for fine-tuning mental health chatbots.

## Source Datasets

- [Gragroo/psy_conv_v1_gpt1](https://huggingface.co/datasets/Gragroo/psy_conv_v1_gpt1)
- [Gragroo/psy_conv_v1_gpt2](https://huggingface.co/datasets/Gragroo/psy_conv_v1_gpt2)
- [Gragroo/psy_conv_v1_gpt3](https://huggingface.co/datasets/Gragroo/psy_conv_v1_gpt3)
- [psycode1/psyset](https://huggingface.co/datasets/psycode1/psyset)
- [Gragroo/psychology-question-answer_psygpt_format](https://huggingface.co/datasets/Gragroo/psychology-question-answer_psygpt_format)

## Cleaning Process

- Converted to standard user/assistant format
- Removed duplicates
- Filtered out harmful content
- Kept therapeutic-relevant responses
